## 2. Core Concept: Parametric Micro-Insurance
## 1. Executive Summary

DORA operates on a parametric insurance model where payouts are triggered based on predefined real-world conditions rather than manual claim submissions.
**DORA (Disruption-Optimized Risk Assurance)** is an AI-powered parametric micro-insurance platform exclusively designed to protect the income of **Quick Commerce (Q-commerce) delivery partners**.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Event-based triggers
- No paperwork or claim filing
- Instant payout execution
- Transparent and predictable logic

### 6.1 Anomaly Detection in Claims

If a significant drop is detected, the process proceeds.
#### Individual Anomaly Detection (Isolation Forest - Roadmap)

**Step 7: Fraud and Validation Layer**
$$
\text{Fraud Score} = \text{IsolationForest}(\mathbf{X}_{\text{behavior}})
$$

Checks include:
- GPS authenticity
- Activity consistency
- Time correlation
- Behavioral anomalies
Where $\mathbf{X}_{\text{behavior}}$ includes:
- GPS coordinates (last 10 pings)
- Speed between pings
- Time at each location
- App interaction frequency

**Step 8: Payout Calculation**
#### Cluster Anomaly Detection

- Loss is calculated
- Coverage percentage is applied
- Caps are enforced
$$
\text{Anomaly Score}_c = \frac{\text{Claim Count}_c}{\text{Expected Claim Count}_c}
$$

**Step 9: Instant Payout to Wallet and Weekly Settlement**
**Rule:** If $\text{Anomaly Score}_c > 3\sigma$ above historical mean → Trigger cluster investigation.

- The calculated payout amount is instantly credited to the user's in-app DORA wallet. This process can be triggered multiple times a day as disruption events are detected and validated.
- Users can view their real-time wallet balance in the app.
- Withdrawals from the DORA wallet to the user's linked bank or UPI account are processed on a fixed, pre-defined schedule such as every Monday at 10:00 AM or twice a week based on the user's policy terms. This encourages users to treat the wallet as a protected savings buffer and optimizes the payout reconciliation process.
- The user is notified when a payout is added to their wallet and when the balance is withdrawn to their bank.
